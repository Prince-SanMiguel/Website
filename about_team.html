The Team


Prince San Miguel

| Founder | 20 years old

As the founder of Online Examination System, Prince is passionate about creating efficient

and user-friendly solutions. With a background in software development, Prince focuses on building the core functionality of the platform

ensuring it’s reliable, secure, and able to handle the demands of online testing. His dedication to quality and streamlined processes drives

drives the technical backbone of the system, enabling it to meet the evolving needs of teachers and students alike.



Raven Rillera

| Co-founder | 21 years old

Raven, a co-founder, brings a creative and user-centered approach to the Online Examination System.

With a knack for design and an eye for detail, he oversees the user experience, making sure the platform is intuitive and easy to navigate.

Raven’s background in IT, combined with his understanding of educational challenges, shapes his work, keeping the needs of educators and students

at the forefront of every design choice. Together with Prince, he aims to make digital exams simpler and more accessible for all.
